Support

Contact

Write to tarun@ecourtsindia.com. Please include your phone or tablet model and the app version (shown on the Settings screen) so we can help faster.

Common questions

The app shows old content even after I updated

The catalog refreshes once per app launch when network is available. Force-quit and reopen the app, or wait a few seconds on the home screen.

I don't see Hindi characters rendering correctly

The app bundles its own Devanagari fonts. If text looks wrong, please report your device and OS version — this should not happen.

How do I change the text size?

Tap the gear icon on the reader screen, then pick Small, Medium, or Large.

My favorites disappeared

Favorites are stored only on your device. They are lost if the app is uninstalled or if device storage is cleared. We don't keep server-side copies.

Can I suggest a new bhajan or correction?

Yes — please email us with the title, lyrics if you have them, and your source.

Is the app free of ads and tracking?

Yes. No ads, no analytics, no tracking SDKs. See the privacy policy for details.
