Jain Bhajans

A quiet reader for Jain devotional songs — bilingual, offline-first, and free.

Jain Bhajans is a mobile app for reading and singing along to traditional bhajans in Devanagari Hindi, with romanized transliteration and English meaning on every line. The catalog ships with the app and updates automatically over the air.

What's inside

  • Hindi (देवनागरी), transliteration, and meaning side by side
  • Per-verse word-by-word translation
  • Favorites and adjustable text size
  • Fully offline once installed
  • No accounts, no ads, no tracking
